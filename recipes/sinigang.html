Sinigang

Description

Sinigang na baboy is a traditional Filipino sour soup made with pork and fresh vegetables. Its signature tangy taste comes from tamarind or sinigang mix, making it a comforting and refreshing meal.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Pork liempo
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 pack sinigang mix
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 radish
  • 10 string beans
  • 2 eggplant
  • kangkong leaves
  • green chili peppers
  • fish sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pork until tender.
  2. Add onions and tomatoes.
  3. Stir in sinigang mix.
  4. Add radish and cook.
  5. Add eggplant and string beans.
  6. Add chili peppers.
  7. Add kangkong
  8. Season with fish sauce and serve.

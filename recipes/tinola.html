Tinolang Manok

Description

Tinolang Manok is a light and healthy Filipino chicken soup cooked with, green papaya, and leafy vegetables such as malunggay or chili leaves. It is known for its comforting and mildly savory broth.

Ingredients

  • 1kg chicken
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 thumb ginger
  • 1 green papaya
  • Malunggay or chili leaves
  • Fish sauce
  • Water
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Saute garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Add chicken and cook.
  3. Add water and simmer.
  4. Add green papaya.
  5. Cook until tender.
  6. Add leafy vegetables.
  7. Serve hot.

Chicken Adobo

Description

Chicken Adobo is one of the most iconic Filipino dishes. It is made by simmering chicken soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves and black peppercorns, creating a savory, tangy, and slightly garlicky flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tsp cooking oil
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil and saute garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour soy sauce and cook for 3 mins.
  4. Add vinegar without stirring and let it boil.
  5. Add water, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
  6. Simmer until chicken becomes tender.
  7. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve with steamed rice.
